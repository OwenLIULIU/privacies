Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

ThreadFuel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Content

  • Topic Descriptions: Text you enter to generate copywriting content
  • Tone Preferences: Your selected writing style preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered copywriting content based on your requests
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your topic descriptions are sent to AI service providers solely for the purpose of generating content. These requests are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

User-generated content is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers. App preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy

ThreadFuel is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
